Frequently Asked Questions

How do I extract a recipe?

Simply paste the URL of any recipe page into FetchDish. The app automatically detects the recipe content, extracts the title, ingredients, and instructions, and saves it to your library. No manual copying required.

What is FetchDish Pro?

FetchDish Pro is a one-time purchase of $19.99 that unlocks unlimited recipe storage, auto-scroll cooking mode, and all future premium features. No subscriptions, no recurring charges -- you pay once and it's yours forever.

How do I restore my purchases?

Open the app and go to Settings, then tap "Restore Purchases." This will re-activate your Pro unlock on any device signed in with the same Apple ID. If you run into trouble, make sure you're connected to the internet and signed in to the App Store.

Which websites are supported?

FetchDish works with most recipe websites that use standard structured recipe markup (such as Schema.org or JSON-LD). This covers the vast majority of popular cooking sites. If a particular site doesn't work, feel free to let us know and we'll look into adding support.
