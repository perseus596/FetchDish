Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 29, 2026

Overview

FetchDish is an ad-free recipe saver app for macOS. Your privacy matters, and this policy explains how little data we collect -- which is none.

No Personal Data Collected

FetchDish does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There is no account creation, no sign-up process, and no login required to use the app.

Local Storage Only

All recipes you save are stored locally on your device. Your data never leaves your computer. FetchDish has no server, no cloud sync, and no remote database.

No Analytics or Tracking

FetchDish contains no analytics SDKs, no tracking pixels, and no telemetry of any kind. We do not monitor how you use the app.

Network Access

FetchDish accesses the internet only when you explicitly provide a URL to extract a recipe from a webpage. The app fetches the content of that page solely to parse recipe data. No other network requests are made.

No Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties. There is no data to share.

Children's Privacy

FetchDish does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at quieresmac@gmail.com.
